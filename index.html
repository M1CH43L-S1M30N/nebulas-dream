In the year 2077, Earth is no longer inhabitable. The only hope for humanity lies in Genesis Two. A distant planet, well outside our solar system in another galaxy called the Nebula Galaxy. Families that cannot afford the official SpaceX flight, have to craft a makeshift spacecraft and traverse the stars alone. With such a primitive design the journey is long. Very long. With abundant resources one the spacecraft, each person’s only obstacle to Genesis Two is, maintaining one’s sanity.
